About

Zynex is a results-driven digital marketing agency passionate about helping businesses succeed in the online world.

DIGITIZE. INCENTIVIZE. GROW.

Founded in 2018, Mysearch Global Rewards transitioned into MSGR - a leading IT solutions company with offices in INDIA, DUBAI & UNITED STATES. Led by a passionate team of IT professionals with over two decades' experience in the industry spanning over a dozen countries across the globe, MSGR sharpened our proficiency in digital innovation & disruption.

We began our operations in Dubai and soon launched an office in Trivandrum, India. Within 5 years of inception, MSGR garnered applause and patronage from renowned organizations for our excellence and expertise in bespoke digital solutions leading to us becoming the

best digital marketing company in Trivandrum & Dubai.

At the end of the day, the people at MSGR simply like to watch businesses grow with our help. To achieve this, we use innovative ideas that facilitate strategic product/service design while ensuring a healthy business ecosystem. We believe combining creativity with just the right choice of technology grants desired results while helping businesses stay ahead of the curve.
